# Project 16 – Business Intelligence Dashboard

Enterprise Business Intelligence Dashboard using ELK Stack for executive reporting, revenue analytics, financial performance monitoring, customer insights, and strategic decision support.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates an enterprise-grade Business Intelligence platform.

The solution provides:

- Business Intelligence
- Revenue Analytics
- Financial Reporting
- Customer Analytics
- Executive Dashboards
- Strategic KPI Monitoring
- Performance Analysis
- Decision Support Analytics

The platform is designed for:

- CEOs
- CFOs
- CIOs
- Business Executives
- PMO Leaders
- Strategy Departments

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations require accurate business intelligence to support strategic decisions.

Without centralized analytics:

- Executive visibility is limited
- Revenue trends are unclear
- Customer insights are fragmented
- Strategic planning becomes difficult

This project provides a unified executive intelligence platform.
