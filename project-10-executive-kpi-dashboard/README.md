# Project 10 – Executive KPI Dashboard

Enterprise Executive KPI Dashboard using ELK Stack for business performance monitoring, executive reporting, strategic analytics, and organizational visibility.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates a C-Level Executive Dashboard that provides real-time visibility into organizational performance.

The platform provides:

- Executive KPIs
- Revenue Analytics
- Financial Performance
- Customer Analytics
- Operational Performance
- Strategic Objectives Tracking
- Executive Reporting

The solution is designed for:

- CEOs
- CIOs
- CFOs
- Executive Directors
- PMO Leaders
- Business Executives

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations require centralized visibility into critical business metrics.

Without executive dashboards:

- Strategic decisions become slower
- KPI visibility is limited
- Revenue risks remain unnoticed
- Executive reporting becomes manual

This project provides executive-level business intelligence.
