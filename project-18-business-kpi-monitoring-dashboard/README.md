# Project 18 – Business KPI Monitoring Dashboard

Enterprise Business KPI Monitoring Dashboard using ELK Stack for strategic performance management, executive scorecards, balanced scorecards, and organizational KPI tracking.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates enterprise-level KPI monitoring and performance management.

The platform provides:

- Business KPI Monitoring
- Balanced Scorecards
- Strategic KPI Tracking
- Department Performance Monitoring
- Executive Scorecards
- Organizational Performance Analytics
- Corporate Performance Management

The solution is designed for:

- CEOs
- PMO Leaders
- Strategy Departments
- Business Executives
- Operations Managers
- Performance Management Teams

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations rely on KPIs to measure success.

Without KPI monitoring:

- Strategic goals become difficult to track
- Performance issues remain hidden
- Executive visibility decreases
- Decision-making becomes reactive

This project provides centralized KPI visibility across the organization.
